Essentials

Master the essential camera controls that form the foundation of professional photography. Understanding aperture, shutter speed, and ISO will give you complete creative control over your images. These settings work together to create the exposure triangle, which is fundamental to achieving the exact look you want in your photographs. When working with AI-enhanced photography tools, having a solid grasp of these basics allows you to make informed decisions about how the AI should process your images.
