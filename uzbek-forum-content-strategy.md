# Uzbek Forum

**Content Strategy & Persona-Based Messaging**

---

## Personas

### Persona 1: IFI Policy Analyst / Bank Stakeholder

**Who:** EBRD/IFC/IPAM staff, ESG officers, policy teams, consultants

**Goals:** Risk mitigation, credible evidence, clear remedy asks

**Pain Points:** Noise, vague allegations, no concrete next step

**Content Preferences:**
- Evidence-stacked threads
- Case timelines & PDFs
- Joint CSO letters
- "What policy lever to pull"

**Channels:**
- X (primary)
- LinkedIn
- Website PDFs

**Language & Tone:** English, technical, precise; cite articles/paras/loan IDs

**CTA:** "Meet re: remedy options" • "Adopt X clause" • "Open investigation"

---

### Persona 2: Brand/Buyer ESG Lead

**Who:** Compliance & sourcing heads in EU/US brands (Textiles/Apparel/Silk)

**Goals:** Supplier assurance, reputational cover, practical guidance

**Pain Points:** Ambiguity on how to source responsibly post-boycott

**Content Preferences:**
- Quick due-diligence checklists
- Risk heatmaps
- Supplier Q&As
- 2-min explainers

**Channels:**
- X (tag brands)
- Website "Buyer Briefs"
- YouTube shorts

**Language & Tone:** English; business-risk framing, "do/don't" lists

**CTA:** "Adopt this 5-point buyer policy" • "Join stakeholder call"

---

### Persona 3: Investigative Journalist / Policy Reporter

**Who:** The Diplomat, OCCRP-adjacent, Brussels/London press

**Goals:** Verified numbers, quotes, human sources, visuals

**Pain Points:** Thin sourcing, slow replies, lack of visuals

**Content Preferences:**
- Media kits
- Quotable stats ("27,928 users targeted")
- Timelines & B-roll
- Contact for witnesses

**Channels:**
- X (DM open)
- YouTube (archival)
- Press page

**Language & Tone:** English, crisp; "5 facts + one quote"

**CTA:** "Press kit" • "Embargoed report snippet" • "Interview our researcher"

---

### Persona 4: Domestic Civic Actor

**Who:** Farmers, co-op organizers, bloggers, local CSOs (UZ/RU)

**Goals:** Practical rights info, what to do next, where to get help; visibility

**Pain Points:** Legal jargon, fear, platforms blocked, long PDFs

**Content Preferences:**
- Step-by-step "What to do if..."
- Numbered Telegram posts
- Voice notes & short clips
- Co-op case updates

**Channels:**
- Telegram (primary)
- Facebook
- YouTube explainers

**Language & Tone:** Uzbek (Cyr/Cyr+Lat) + Russian; plain language

**CTA:** "Forward to group" • "Document incident via this form" • "Contact helpline"

---

### Persona 5: Diaspora & Advocacy Allies

**Who:** Uzbek diaspora, students, solidarity groups

**Goals:** Clear narrative, concrete actions, events to attend

**Pain Points:** Abstract policy talk, no tangible action

**Content Preferences:**
- Shareable carousels
- Petition moments
- "Email-your-MP" templates
- Event invites

**Channels:**
- Facebook
- Telegram
- X

**Language & Tone:** EN + RU/UZ for reach; motivating tone

**CTA:** "Share this" • "Sign letter" • "Turn up on [date]"

---

### Persona 6: Donor / Foundation Program Officer

**Who:** Human rights & business/human-rights funders

**Goals:** Impact evidence, theory of change, unique value

**Pain Points:** Output ≠ outcome; unclear differentiation from peers

**Content Preferences:**
- Quarterly outcome snapshots
- "What changed at IFI X"
- Influence maps
- Case studies

**Channels:**
- X for visibility
- Direct newsletters
- Board-ready PDFs

**Language & Tone:** English; outcomes, not just activities

**CTA:** "Read outcome memo" • "Join quarterly briefing"

---

### Persona 7: General International Public

**Who:** HR-curious broad audience encountering content via shares

**Goals:** Understand the 'why' quickly; human stories

**Pain Points:** Acronyms, policy alphabet soup

**Content Preferences:**
- 60–90s subtitled videos
- "Before/After" stories
- Simple stat cards

**Channels:**
- YouTube shorts
- Facebook
- X threads

**Language & Tone:** English first; avoid jargon

**CTA:** "Watch/Share" • "Subscribe" • "Donate"

## Content Pipeline Strategy

### Content Series

#### "MDB Minutes"
60-sec videos decoding EBRD/IFC acronyms and what "remedy" really means (EN captions, optional UZ VO)

#### "Farmer Rights 101" on Telegram
8-post numbered series (UZ) with templates and contacts

#### "Buyer Briefs"
1-page PDFs with do/don't for sourcing in Uzbekistan's cotton/silk

#### Press Micro-kits
Per report: 5 facts + 2 quotes + B-roll links

#### #HumanRightsAlerts
Keep threading on X; cross-post condensed to Bluesky; compile monthly digest

### Platform Mix Strategy

- **X/Bluesky:** 80/20 policy-first (strongest policy/IFI lane)
- **Telegram:** 40/60 policy/public, leaning UZ/RU practical posts
- **Facebook:** 60/40; slightly more public-facing than X but evidence-led
- **YouTube:** 40/60 with short explainers + archival docs (EN captions, UZ voice-over)
